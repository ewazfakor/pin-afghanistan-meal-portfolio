## Indicator Definitions & Measurement Notes

### OUT-1.1: Food Consumption Score (FCS)
**Definition:** A weighted score based on dietary diversity and food frequency over the past 7 days. Score range 0–112. FCS ≥ 35 indicates acceptable food consumption.

**Calculation:**
```
FCS = (Cereal × 2) + (Pulses × 3) + (Vegetables × 1) + (Fruit × 1) + (Meat × 4) + (Milk × 4) + (Sugar × 0.5) + (Oil × 0.5)
```

**Quality Checks:**
- Sum of days across all food groups must equal ≤7 per group
- Total FCS must fall between 0 and 112
- Enumerators must read food group definitions verbatim

### OUT-1.2: reduced Coping Strategies Index (rCSI)
**Definition:** Counts frequency of 5 stress/crisis coping behaviors over the past 7 days. Score range 0–56. rCSI ≤ 18 indicates low coping.

**Calculation:**
```
rCSI = (Rations × 1) + (Borrow × 2) + (Reduce Meals × 1) + (Restrict Adult × 3) + (Send Children to Eat Elsewhere × 2)
```

### OP-1.2: Kitchen Garden Survival
**Definition:** A kitchen garden is "maintained" if ≥3 vegetable varieties are actively growing at the time of monitoring visit, with visible care (watering, weeding) in the past 14 days.

**Verification:**
- Physical visit with photo documentation
- GPS coordinates matched to beneficiary registration
- Minimum 3 angles: wide plot, close-up plants, beneficiary with garden

### OP-2.1: Canal/Kareze Rehabilitation
**Definition:** Length in meters of canal or kareze segments cleared, repaired, or lined through Cash-for-Work labor, verified by engineering measurement.

**Verification:**
- Pre-rehabilitation and post-rehabilitation GPS-tagged photos
- Engineering sign-off on technical specifications
- CFW attendance records cross-checked against payment logs
